## Nha vo dich hang ga Nhat Ban Masuda Riku tham gia tran dau ngay 8/6 tai Ariake, cung "tranh tai" voi Tenshin Nasukawa, Tomoya Tsuboi va nhung nguoi khac.

![](/images/20250430-04301253-sph-000-2-view.webp)

Masuda Riku

Su kien boxing chuyen nghiep "PRIME VIDEO BOXING 13" se dien ra vao ngay 8 thang 6 tai Ariake Coliseum, Tokyo. Cac tran dau noi bat bao gom:

*   Tran thong nhat dai vo dich the gioi hang ga WBC va IBF (duoi 53.5 kg), 12 hiep: Nha vo dich WBC Junto Nakatani doi dau nha vo dich IBF Ryosuke Nishida.
*   Tran hang ga, 10 hiep: Tenshin Nasukawa doi dau Victor Santillan.
*   Tran tranh dai vo dich hang ga WBO Asia Pacific (P) con trong, 10 hiep: Tomoya Tsuboi doi dau Van Thao Tran.
*   Tran hang ga, 10 hiep: Riku Masuda doi dau Michel Banquez.

Phong tap Teiken Gym da cong bo tren mang xa hoi vao ngay 30 rang nha vo dich hang ga Nhat Ban cua ho, Riku Masuda, da quyet dinh tham gia su kien "PRIME VIDEO BOXING 13" vao ngay 8 thang 6 tai Ariake Coliseum, Tokyo. Masuda se thi dau trong mot tran khong tranh đai hang ga keo dai 10 hiep voi Michel Banquez (Venezuela), nguoi xep thu 11 hang ga the gioi cua WBA.

Thanh tich thi dau: Masuda 27 tuoi, co 7 tran thang (7 KOs) va 1 tran thua. Banquez 34 tuoi, co 23 tran thang (15 KOs) va 5 tran thua.

Masuda ra mat chuyen nghiep vao thang 7 nam 2022. Anh gianh dai vo dich hang ga Nhat Ban vao thang 7 nam 2024 sau khi ha guc Fumiya Tomiyuki (Watanabe) o hiep thu 4. Vao thang 3 nam nay, anh da bao ve thanh cong dai vo dich lan thu hai bang cach danh bai Kaito Matsumoto (VADY) bang TKO o hiep thu 6.

Banquez ra mat chuyen nghiep vao thang 12 nam 2015 va co chuoi 14 tran thang. Anh gianh đai vo dich the gioi hang ga IBO (khong duoc Nhat Ban cong nhan) vao thang 7 nam 2019. Vao thang 7 nam 2022, anh da doi dau voi Ramon Cardenas (My), nguoi hien dang xep thu nhat hang super bantamweight cua WBA va se thach dau nha vo dich thong nhat 4 dai the gioi hang super bantamweight Naoya Inoue (Ohashi) tai Las Vegas, My vao ngay 4 thang 5 toi (sang 5 thang 5 theo gio Nhat Ban). Banquez la mot vo si thuan tay phai da tung thua Cardenas bang quyet dinh dong thuan (0-3).

Tai su kien nay, ngoai tran thong nhat dai giua nha vo dich hang ga WBC Junto Nakatani (M.T) va nha vo dich IBF Ryosuke Nishida (Six Island), nha vo dich hang ga WBC xep thu nhat Tenshin Nasukawa se thi dau tran khong tranh đai 10 hiep voi Victor Santillan (Cong hoa Dominica), nguoi xep thu 6 hang ga cua WBA, nhu mot "tran chien tien the gioi". Ngoai ra, Tomoya Tsuboi (Teiken), nguoi xep thu hai hang ga WBO Asia P va la nguoi Nhat Ban dau tien gianh huy chuong vang tai Giai vo dich the gioi nam 2021, se doi dau voi Van Thao Tran (Viet Nam), nguoi xep thu nhat hang ga, trong tran tranh đai WBO Asia P con trong ma Nasukawa da tu bo.

Ve vao cua se bat dau mo ban rong rai tu 18:00 ngay 1 thang 5.

Cac tran dau se duoc truyen hinh truc tiep doc quyen tren Prime Video.
