# "Ho da rat tuyet voi" - Asada, nguoi tham gia LIV, va Kozuma, nguoi hua quay tro lai, noi ve su chan dong! "Long mon" thi dau quoc te khai mac tai Caledonian GC, Chiba [Asian Tour]

![](/images/20260310-00010010-minnagolf-000-2-view.webp)

Asada Yosuke (trai) va Kozuma Jinichiro tham gia hop bao qua Zoom

Khi "International Series Japan" - giai mo man cua "International Series" nam 2026 - sap dien ra tai Nhat Ban vao thang toi, mot buoi hop bao gioi thieu da duoc to chuc tai Caledonian Golf Club, tinh Chiba. "International Series" la loai giai dau co giai thuong cao do Asian Tour to chuc, duoc PIF Saudi International (tap doan dau tu vao LIV Golf) ho tro. Giai thuong cao hon cac tran dau thong thuong, va vao nam 2025, hai nguoi dung dau bang xep hang theo nam se gianh duoc quyen tham du LIV Golf League vao nam sau, do do cac tay golf xuat sac tren khap the gioi se tu hop. Giai dau quan trong nay, tranh ve thi dau giac mo, se dien ra tu ngay 2 den ngay 5 thang 4.
[Tu "thu gian" den tham gia LIV. Asada Yosuke ke ve tam ve "ngoai du tinh" sang nuoc ngoai](https://www.golfdigest-minna.jp/_ct/17818238)

Trong ngay hom nay, tu Caledonian GC va cac dia diem khac nhau, Asada Yosuke da tham gia hop bao qua Zoom tu san thi dau LIV Golf Singapore, con Kozuma Jinichiro, nguoi xep thu 7 tai giai dau Nhat Ban nam ngoai, da tham gia tu nha cua minh o Nhat Ban.
Asada, nguoi da dung thu hai bang xep hang cua chuoi giai dau nam ngoai va tham gia LIV Golf theo the thuc toan thoi gian tu mua giai nam nay, nhin lai tinh hinh hien tai va noi: "Toi chua bao gio nghi den dieu nay."
"Toi thuc su hanh phuc khi co gang trong chuoi giai dau va ket qua la duoc tham gia LIV. Bay gio, sau khi da thi dau 3 giai LIV, dieu quan trong nhat la toi cam thay rat vui. Toi may man duoc choi cung voi nhung tay golf co trinh do cao."
Asada da tiet lo nhung gi anh da nghe tu Kozuma ve trinh do cua LIV Golf.
"Jin (Kozuma) luon noi voi toi rang 'Ho rat tuyet voi'. Khi bat dau thi dau, toi da nhan ra rang co rat nhieu nguoi co the di xa, va khong chi tam xa ma ho con co ky nang choi golf doc dao o muc do cao. Toi da hieu rang co nhieu kieu nguoi ma ban khong gap o tour golf Nhat Ban."
Kozuma, nguoi cung co muc tieu tro lai LIV Golf thong qua chuoi giai dau nay, cung da noi ve suc manh cua cac tay golf hang dau the gioi mot cach cu the.
"Ngay ca khi mot ngay thi dau khong tot, ho luon co the co mot diem so tuyet voi va len top. Dac biet, cuoc vuot len o cuoi vong dau cuoi cung la rat khung khiep. Ban than toi, toi cam thay trinh do cua minh da tang len sau khi thi dau 2 nam tai LIV Golf, va kinh nghiem xep thu hai tai LIV Golf Dallas nam ngoai da mang lai cho toi su tu tin lon. Muon tro lai LIV Golf trong mua giai nay, toi da quyet dinh tap trung vao International Series."
Ngoai ra, ve y nghia cua giai dau nay khi duoc to chuc tai Nhat Ban, anh noi: "Giai thuong cao hon cac tran dau o Nhat Ban, va cac tay golf co trinh do cao se tu hop. Doi voi cac tay golf Nhat Ban muon thi dau o nuoc ngoai, day la mot giai dau co anh huong rat tot, vi no ket noi con duong den the gioi." Tren thuc te, tong giai thuong du kien la 2 trieu USD (khoang 315 trieu JPY). Giai thuong vo dich chua duoc cong bo, nhung du kien se co giai thuong cao tuong duong 52 trieu JPY nhu nam ngoai.
International Series nam nay, bat dau voi giai dau tai Nhat Ban, se tiep tuc di chuyen khap the gioi den Singapore, Vietnam, Morocco, India, China, va ket thuc tai Saudi Arabia. Cuoc dua sinh ton, noi cac tay golf Nhat Ban va cac doi thu manh tren the gioi tranh gianh ve tham du LIV Golf danh cho nguoi dung dau va thu hai cua chuoi giai, se bat dau vao ngay 2 thang 4, tai vung dat Chiba.
