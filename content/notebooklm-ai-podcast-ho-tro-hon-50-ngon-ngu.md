# NotebookLM AI Podcast Ho Tro Hon 50 Ngon Ngu, Bao Gom Ca Tieng Viet

![](/images/20250430-35232431-zdnet-000-2-view.webp)

NotebookLM, mot so tay ghi chep thuc nghiem su dung tri tue nhan tao (AI) cua Google, da tro nen pho bien nho tinh nang "Audio Overview" cho phep chuyen noi dung van ban thanh podcast voi hai nguoi dan AI. Tinh nang nay co the lam cho cac noi dung phuc tap tro nen de tiep thu hon bang cach bien chung thanh cuoc tro chuyen nhe nhang giua hai nguoi dan, giup nguoi dung de hieu hon. No dac biet phu hop de hoc hoac hieu ro cac noi dung kho. Va bay gio, tinh nang nay da mo rong ho tro them nhieu ngon ngu hon.

Google thong bao vao ngay 25 thang 4 (gio My) rang ho da tang so luong ngon ngu duoc ho tro boi tinh nang Audio Overview dua tren Gemini 2.5 Pro len hon 50. Danh sach cac ngon ngu nay bao gom nhieu loai tieng khac nhau nhu Tay Ban Nha va Tieng Phi. Video YouTube duoi day mo ta cach hai nguoi dan AI cua Audio Overview giai thich ve thong bao nay.

Voi ban cap nhat lan nay, mot tuy chon moi ten la "Output Language" (Ngon ngu dau ra) da duoc them vao phan cai dat cua NotebookLM. Tuy chon nay cho phep nguoi dung thay doi ngon ngu cua am thanh va phan hoi chat bat cu luc nao, giup viec tao noi dung bang cac ngon ngu khac tro nen de dang.

Cac truong hop su dung duoc mong doi bao gom viec don gian hoa noi dung cho nguoi khong phai la nguoi ban dia, xuat noi dung bang ngon ngu dang hoc de phuc vu viec hoc, hoac dich cac noi dung nhu nghien cuu sang ngon ngu me de.

Duoi day la danh sach tat ca cac ngon ngu duoc ho tro: Tieng Phi, Tieng A Rap, Tieng Azerbaijan, Tieng Bungary, Tieng Bengali, Tieng Catalan, Tieng Sec, Tieng Dan Mach, Tieng Duc, Tieng Hy Lap, Tieng Tay Ban Nha (Chau Au), Tieng Tay Ban Nha (My Latin), Tieng Tay Ban Nha (Mexico), Tieng Estonia, Tieng Basque, Tieng Ba Tu, Tieng Phan Lan, Tieng Philippines, Tieng Phap (Chau Au), Tieng Phap (Canada), Tieng Galicia, Tieng Gujarati, Tieng Hindi, Tieng Croatia, Tieng Haiti Creole, Tieng Hungari, Tieng Armenia, Tieng Indonesia, Tieng Iceland, Tieng Y, Tieng Do Thai, Tieng Nhat, Tieng Java, Tieng Georgia, Tieng Kannada, Tieng Han Quoc, Tieng Konkani, Tieng Latin, Tieng Litva, Tieng Latvia, Tieng Maithili, Tieng Macedonia, Tieng Malayalam, Tieng Marathi, Tieng Ma Lai, Tieng Myanmar, Tieng Nepal, Tieng Ha Lan, Tieng Na Uy (Nynorsk), Tieng Na Uy (Bokmal), Tieng Oriya, Tieng Punjabi, Tieng Ba Lan, Tieng Pashto, Tieng Bo Dao Nha (Brazil), Tieng Bo Dao Nha (Bo Dao Nha), Tieng Rumani, Tieng Nga, Tieng Sindhi, Tieng Sinhala, Tieng Slovakia, Tieng Slovenia, Tieng Albania, Tieng Serbia (chu Cyrillic), Tieng Thuy Dien, Tieng Swahili, Tieng Tamil, Tieng Telugu, Tieng Thai, Tieng Tho Nhi Ky, Tieng Ukraine, Tieng Urdu, Tieng Viet, Tieng Trung (Gian the), Tieng Trung (Truyen thong).

Thong bao nay rat dung luc, boi Google cung da cong bo vao ngay 17 rang ho se cung cap goi "Google One AI Premium" tri gia 19,99 USD moi thang (2900 Yen tai Nhat Ban) mien phi tam thoi cho sinh vien dai hoc tai My. Dang ky goi nay cung cho phep nguoi dung su dung cac tinh nang cua "NotebookLM Plus" tra phi. NotebookLM Plus cung cap cac tinh nang doc quyen danh cho nguoi dang ky, va tang gap 5 lan gioi han su dung tren moi so tay doi voi mot so tinh nang chinh cua NotebookLM (Audio Overview, So tay, truy van, nguon).
