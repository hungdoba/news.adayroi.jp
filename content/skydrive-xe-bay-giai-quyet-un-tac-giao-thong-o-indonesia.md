# SkyDrive "xe bay" giai quyet un tac giao thong o Indonesia

![](/images/20260220-00000005-nna_kyodo-000-1-view.webp)

CEO Tomohiro Fukuzawa cua SkyDrive phat bieu tai mot buoi hop bao tai Hiiep Hoi Phong Vien Nuoc Ngoai Nhat Ban (FCCJ) o Tokyo, ngay 18 thang 2 (Anh: NNA)

**TOKYO** – SkyDrive, mot cong ty phat trien "xe bay", cho biet se dat duoc chung nhan kieu dang tai Nhat Ban va My, huong toi bat dau khai thac dich vu vao nam 2028. Tai Indonesia, SkyDrive se hop tac voi mot doanh nghiep dia phuong de gop phan giai quyet van de un tac giao thong.

**Dat hang truoc the gioi**

Tinh den thang 12 nam 2025, SkyDrive da nhan duoc 415 don dat hang truoc tren toan the gioi tai 8 quoc gia.

Tai tieu vuong quoc Arab Thong nhat (UAE), ngoai viec hop tac voi chinh phu, cong ty da nhan duoc don dat hang truoc toi da 50 chiec tu mot cong ty cho thue truc thang o Dubai.

**Moi rong kinh doanh quoc te**

CEO Tomohiro Fukuzawa cung bay to y dinh day nhanh viec mo rong kinh doanh ra nuoc ngoai, dac biet la tai Dong Nam A va UAE.

Tai Dong Nam A, SkyDrive se hop tac voi White Sky Aviation, mot cong ty van chuyen truc thang lon cua Indonesia, de giai quyet cac van de xa hoi nhu un tac giao thong. Cong ty huong toi khai thac nhu cau di chuyen giua san bay va khu vuc thanh pho, cung nhu xay dung cac dich vu di chuyen moi bao gom chuyen tiep tu mat dat len bau troi.

SkyDrive cung dang tien hanh hop tac va nhan don dat hang truoc tai Viet Nam, Han Quoc, Dai Loan, Thai Lan va cac quoc gia khac.

Cong ty cung hop tac voi cac doanh nghiep An Do trong viec phat trien linh kien, mo rong chuoi cung ung.

**Tiet kiem nang luong va de bao tri**

Cac thiet bi cua SkyDrive hoan toan chay bang dien, co do on nho va it bo phan, do do de bao tri.

Thiet bi co suc chua 3 nguoi (1 phi cong, 2 hanh khach), toc do hanh trinh 100 km/gio, va tam bay xa tu 15-40 km.

Theo ong Fukuzawa, thiet ke se dam bao kha nang tiep tuc bay ngay ca khi 2 trong so 12 canh quat bi ngung hoat dong.

Ve ung dung, ong cho biet "Hien tai, viec su dung chu yeu la trong van chuyen hang hoa, du lich va van chuyen y te khan cap."

**Tang cuong san xuat va quy dong von**

Ve mat san xuat, SkyDrive hop tac voi Suzuki va se co kha nang san xuat quy mo 100 chiec/nam tai nha may cua tap doan Suzuki tai Iwata, Shizuoka.

Tinh den thang 12 nam 2025, tong so von huy dong duoc cua cong ty vuot qua 43 ty yen.

**Bay thu nghiem tai Tokyo**

SkyDrive se tien hanh bay thu nghiem tai Tokyo Big Sight tu ngay 24 den 28 thang 11. Cac chuyen bay du kien dien ra hai lan moi ngay (9:30 sang va 12:05 trua). Viec xem bay la mien phi.
